Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Youke Union (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "the platform") attaches great importance to your privacy protection. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Types of Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Information you provide during registration, including your name, email address, phone number, avatar, etc.
  • Learning Records: Your course progress, assignment submissions, exam results, study duration, etc.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, browser type, IP address, and other technical information
  • Usage Data: Your actions on the platform, pages visited, time spent, etc.
  • Communication Information: Content of your communications with us via customer service, feedback, etc.

1.2 Methods of Information Collection

  • Information you provide voluntarily (registration, filling out forms, etc.)
  • Automatically collected via cookies and similar technologies
  • Information obtained through third-party partners
  • Information obtained from public sources

2. Use of Information

2.1 We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Providing Services: To provide you with online learning services, including course recommendations, learning progress tracking, etc.
  • Account Management: Creating and maintaining your account, identity verification, payment processing, etc.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Recommending relevant courses and content based on your learning preferences and history
  • Customer Service: Responding to your inquiries, resolving technical issues, providing technical support
  • Security: Protecting the platform and user security, preventing fraud and malicious activities
  • Improving Services: Analyzing user behavior to improve our products and service quality
  • Marketing: Sending you relevant marketing information (with your consent)

2.2 Legal Basis

We process your personal information based on the following legal grounds:

  • Fulfilling contractual obligations with you
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Based on your explicit consent
  • For legitimate interests

3. Information Sharing

3.1 We Do Not Sell Your Personal Information

We promise not to sell, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties.

3.2 We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Consent: With your explicit consent
  • Service Providers: Sharing with trusted third-party service providers, such as payment processing, data analysis, etc.
  • Legal Requirements: As required by laws, court orders, or government requests
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the legitimate rights and interests of users, the platform, or the public

4. Information Storage

4.1 Storage Location

Your personal information will be stored on secure servers within the People's Republic of China. If cross-border transmission is required, we will strictly comply with relevant laws and regulations.

4.2 Storage Period

  • Account Information: During the existence of your account
  • Learning Records: Retained for the period required by educational management regulations
  • Log Information: Usually retained for 12 months
  • Other Information: Retention period determined according to specific circumstances and legal requirements

5. Information Security

5.1 Security Measures

We use various security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Technical Measures: Data encryption, firewalls, intrusion detection systems, etc.
  • Management Measures: Strict access control, staff training, confidentiality agreements, etc.
  • Physical Measures: Secure data centers, access control, monitoring systems, etc.

5.2 Data Breach Response

If a personal information breach occurs, we will:

  • Immediately initiate an emergency plan to control the situation
  • Promptly assess risks and take remedial measures
  • Report to regulatory authorities as required by law
  • Promptly notify affected users

6. Your Rights

6.1 You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Know: Understand how we process your personal information
  • Right of Access: View the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information under certain conditions
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Restrict our processing of your information under certain circumstances
  • Right to Data Portability: Obtain your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Right to Object: Object to our processing of your personal information

6.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

You can exercise the above rights in the following ways:

  • Log in to your account and manage personal information in settings
  • Contact our customer service team
  • Send an email to: privacy@youkelianmeng.com

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

7.1 Use of Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login status and preferences
  • Analyze website usage and improve user experience
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements
  • Ensure website security and prevent fraud

7.2 Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Disable or delete cookies
  • Set cookie notifications
  • Selectively accept cookies from specific websites

8. Protection of Minors

We attach great importance to the protection of minors' personal information:

  • Children under the age of 14 need guardian consent to use our services
  • We do not actively collect personal information from children under 14
  • If we discover that we have inadvertently collected children's information, we will delete it immediately
  • Guardians may request access, correction, or deletion of children's personal information

9. International Transfers

If your personal information needs to be transferred outside the People's Republic of China, we will:

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Ensure the recipient provides an adequate level of protection
  • Adopt appropriate safeguards, such as standard contractual clauses
  • Obtain your explicit consent when necessary

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. In the event of major changes, we will:

  • Post update notices in prominent positions on the website
  • Notify you by email or in-site messages
  • Obtain your consent in certain cases
  • Recommend that you regularly review the latest version of this policy
