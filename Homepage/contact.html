Frequently Asked Questions

How to register an account?

Click the "Register" button at the top right of the page, fill in the basic information to complete the registration. After registration, you can watch some public courses for free.

How to purchase courses?

Browse the course list, select the course you are interested in, click the "Buy Now" button, and follow the instructions to complete the payment.

Is refund supported?

We offer a 7-day unconditional refund service. If you are not satisfied with the course, you can apply for a refund within 7 days after purchase.

How to obtain a learning certificate?

After completing the course and passing the exam, the system will automatically generate a learning certificate for you. The certificate can be downloaded in your personal center.
