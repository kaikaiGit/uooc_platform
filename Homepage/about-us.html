About Youke Union

We are committed to building China's leading online education ecosystem, connecting high-quality educational resources with learners, making knowledge accessible without boundaries.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Through advanced technology and high-quality educational resources, we provide every learner with a personalized and efficient learning experience, promote educational equity, and make quality education accessible to all.

Our Vision

To become a world-leading online education platform, building an open, shared, and collaborative educational ecosystem, making learning a driving force for lifelong growth for everyone.
